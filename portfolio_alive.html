Stopmotion for school

This was made for college. We had to make a one minute stopmotion movie. First I started thinking about what I could use as a character. When I found some stuff in a box from when I was a kid, I decided to use the fluffy ball and put eyes on it. Then I started working on an idea. I wanted it to be silly and colorful. I think it turned out pretty well.
